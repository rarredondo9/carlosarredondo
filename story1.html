March 10, 2025

This morning I was reminded of Dad’s favorite routine...

He always started his day with a cup of coffee and a smile. I remember how he would hum quietly while reading the newspaper, his presence filling the kitchen with comfort. It’s those small, ordinary moments that I find myself missing the most.

Sometimes I sit in that same chair and pour myself coffee, just to feel close to him. Even though he’s not here, the warmth of those mornings stays with me. They remind me that love lives on in the everyday details.

Hecho con mucho amor❤️
